Гайд по строительству базы

Освойте строительство базы, выбор локации и продвинутые оборонительные стратегии

Location Selection

Choosing the right location is crucial for your base's survival and your resource accessibility.

Optimal Locations

RECOMMENDED

Near Multiple Houses

Easy access to electronics and sheet metal for advanced crafting.

Map Border Placement

One side pre-protected by map boundary, reducing defensive requirements.

Resource Proximity

Close to wood, stone, and iron sources for efficient gathering.

Water Access

Near rivers or other water sources for hydration needs.

Locations to Avoid

AVOID

Inside Houses

Building inside houses angers other players and makes you a target for raids.

High Radiation Areas

Constant health drain without proper protection gear.

High Traffic Areas

Areas with frequent player movement increase raid risk.

Resource-Poor Zones

Areas far from essential resources slow down progression.

Essential Base Components

Every successful base needs these core components for survival and progression.

Stone Walls

Critical: Always use stone walls for defense. Wood walls are too weak against ghouls!

Why Stone Walls:

  • Higher durability against ghoul attacks
  • Better protection against player raids
  • Longer lasting in harsh conditions
  • Required for serious base defense

Multiple Entrances

Safety: Always have multiple entrances to prevent being trapped during raids.

Entrance Strategy:

  • Build at least 2-3 different entrances
  • Place them on different sides of your base
  • Include hidden or secondary exits
  • Consider emergency escape routes

Crafting Stations

Required: Essential crafting stations for progression and survival.

Must-Have Stations:

  • Workbench: Basic crafting (build multiple for teams)
  • Smelter: Metal processing and alloys
  • Research Bench: Technology advancement
  • Tesla Bench: End-game equipment crafting

Storage System

Organization: Multiple chests for organized resource storage.

Storage Tips:

  • Separate chests by material type
  • Label storage areas mentally
  • Keep emergency supplies in secure locations
  • Protect valuable storage from raids

Small Farm

Sustainability: Minimum 3x3 farm for food self-sufficiency.

Farming Benefits:

  • Reliable food source independent of hunting
  • Reduces need for dangerous foraging
  • Provides steady nutrition for health maintenance
  • Can be expanded as base grows

Advanced Defense Strategies

Master the most effective defensive techniques to protect your base from ghouls and players.

Repair Bot Strategy (Ultimate Defense)

The most effective base defense technique using automated repair bots:

Implementation Steps:

  1. Strategic Placement: Place repair bots along your wall perimeter
  2. Protection: Seal each repair bot with wooden slabs to protect them from attacks
  3. Coverage Ratio: Maintain minimum 2 repair bots per 3 walls for effective ghoul protection
  4. Layered Defense: Surround your base with multiple wall layers
  5. Backup Systems: Have spare repair bots ready for replacement

Why This Works:

  • Automated Repair: Walls are constantly maintained without player intervention
  • Ghoul Protection: Effectively counters even large ghoul waves
  • 24/7 Defense: Works even when you're offline or away
  • Cost Effective: Expensive initially but saves resources long-term
Important: Repair bots are expensive to craft but provide the best automated defense available. Protect them at all costs!

Additional Defense Techniques

Wall Layering

Build multiple layers of walls around your base for increased protection.

  • Inner walls protect core structures
  • Outer walls absorb initial damage
  • Creates delay for defensive response
  • Forces attackers to use more resources

Watchtowers

Build elevated positions for better visibility and defensive advantage.

  • Early threat detection
  • Better shooting angles
  • Safe zones during attacks
  • Communication with team members

Maze Layouts

Design confusing pathways to slow down attackers and ghouls.

  • Forces enemies to take longer routes
  • Creates ambush opportunities
  • Confuses raiding players
  • Provides escape route advantages

Trap Systems

Set up defensive traps around your base perimeter.

  • Damage unsuspecting attackers
  • Slow down ghoul advances
  • Psychological deterrent effect
  • Area denial tactics

Base Progression Stages

Understand how to evolve your base from a simple shelter to a fortified stronghold.

Stage 1: Emergency Shelter

EARLY GAME

Priority Items:

  • Basic stone walls for protection
  • Single entrance with door
  • Campfire for cooking and warmth
  • Workbench for basic crafting
  • Storage chest for essential items

Goal: Survive the first few days and establish basic safety.

Stage 2: Functional Base

MID GAME

Expansion Items:

  • Multiple stone wall layers
  • 2-3 entrances for safety
  • Smelter for metal processing
  • Multiple storage chests
  • Small farm (3x3 minimum)
  • Research bench for advancement

Goal: Self-sufficiency and preparation for ghoul waves.

Stage 3: Fortified Stronghold

LATE GAME

Advanced Features:

  • Repair bot defensive network
  • Tesla bench for end-game gear
  • Extensive wall layering
  • Watchtowers and defensive positions
  • Automated feeder system
  • Emergency escape routes

Goal: Withstand any threat and dominate the area.

Team Base Building

Special considerations for building bases with teammates.

Connected Bases

Build individual bases connected by secure tunnels or walls.

Benefits:

  • Individual player autonomy
  • Shared defense network
  • Resource sharing capabilities
  • Redundancy if one base is compromised

Compound Design

Single large base with multiple sections for different team members.

Features:

  • Central crafting area
  • Individual living quarters
  • Shared storage systems
  • Coordinated defense structure

Role Specialization

Assign specific base-building roles to team members.

Roles:

  • Architect: Plans layout and design
  • Builder: Constructs walls and structures
  • Defender: Manages defensive systems
  • Supplier: Gathers building materials

Common Building Mistakes

Using Wood Walls

Wood walls are too weak against ghouls and experienced players. Always use stone for defense.

Single Entrance

Having only one entrance makes you vulnerable to being trapped during raids or attacks.

Poor Location Choice

Building in high-traffic areas or inside houses attracts unwanted attention and conflict.

Inadequate Storage

Not having enough organized storage leads to resource waste and inefficient crafting.

No Defensive Planning

Building without considering ghoul waves and player attacks leads to base destruction.

Ignoring Expansion

Not planning for base growth limits your ability to progress and adapt to threats.
