Essential Early Game Crafts

These are the critical items you need to craft immediately upon spawning to ensure survival.

Hatchet

CRITICAL

Recipe:

  • 2x Stone (your starting stones!)
  • 1x Wood

Purpose: Essential wood gathering tool. Makes wood collection 3x more efficient than using bare hands.

Critical Note: You spawn with exactly 2 stones - never drop these!

Stone Pickaxe

HIGH

Recipe:

  • 1x Stone
  • 1x Wood

Purpose: Mining tool for stone and iron ore. Also serves as a decent melee weapon.

Tip: Essential for gathering the stone and iron needed for advanced crafting.

Workbench

HIGH

Recipe:

  • 4x Wood
  • 2x Stone

Purpose: Unlocks advanced crafting recipes and allows creation of better tools.

Strategy: Build multiple workbenches for team play to speed up crafting.

Campfire

HIGH

Recipe:

  • 3x Wood
  • 1x Stone

Purpose: Cook food and provide warmth during cold nights.

Critical: Essential for surviving temperature drops and making food safe to eat.

Advanced Crafting Stations

These stations unlock higher-tier crafting recipes and are essential for progression.

Smelter

Purpose:

Smelt raw iron ore into usable metal for advanced tools and weapons.

Requirements:

  • Iron ore (mined with pickaxe)
  • Fuel (wood or coal)

Unlocks:

  • Metal tools (axe, pickaxe)
  • Metal weapons
  • Alloys (iron + coal)

Research Bench

Purpose:

Unlock higher-tier recipes and advanced technologies.

Requirements:

  • Electronics (from computers/TVs)
  • Various materials for research

Unlocks:

  • Advanced weapons
  • Protection gear
  • Tesla technology

Tesla Bench

Purpose:

Craft end-game power armor and laser weapons.

Requirements:

  • Shaped uranium (10 for Tesla armor)
  • Advanced electronics
  • Alloys and rare materials

Unlocks:

  • Tesla armor (ultimate protection)
  • Laser SMG (top-tier weapon)
  • Power equipment

Feeder

Purpose:

Automated food source for late-game sustainability.

Requirements:

  • Advanced materials
  • Power source
  • Electronics

Benefits:

  • Eliminates need for manual food gathering
  • Provides consistent nutrition
  • Frees up time for other activities

Essential Materials Guide

Understanding materials and their sources is crucial for efficient crafting.

Basic Materials

Wood

Source: Trees (use hatchet for efficiency)

Minimum stock: 200 at all times

Uses: All basic crafting, fuel, building

Stone

Source: Rock formations (use pickaxe)

Minimum stock: 150 at all times

Uses: Tools, building materials, walls

Iron Ore

Source: Iron deposits (requires pickaxe)

Processing: Smelt in smelter to get iron

Uses: Advanced tools, weapons, alloys

Advanced Materials

Alloys

Recipe: Iron + Coal (in smelter)

Source: Crafted material

Uses: Advanced weapons, high-tier equipment

Electronics

Source: Computers, TVs in buildings

Risk: Often in dangerous areas

Uses: Research, Tesla technology, advanced gear

Shaped Uranium

Source: Advanced crafting processes

Requirement: 10 for Tesla armor

Uses: End-game equipment, power armor

Special Materials

Gasoline

Recipe: Rotten oranges + Sulfur

Creative use: Turn waste into explosives

Uses: Explosives, advanced weapons

Animal Products

Source: Hunting animals

Processing: Cook meat before eating

Uses: Food, crafting materials

Sheet Metal

Source: Houses and buildings

Usage: Advanced building materials

Strategy: Build bases near multiple houses

Important Crafting Tips

Resource Protection

Warning: Anyone can take items from unprotected crafting stations!

  • Guard your crafting stations when in use
  • Store valuable materials in protected chests
  • Build in secure, hidden locations when possible
  • Never leave valuable items unattended

Efficient Crafting

Strategy: Optimize your crafting workflow for maximum efficiency.

  • Batch craft similar items together
  • Keep materials organized in nearby chests
  • Use multiple workbenches for team crafting
  • Plan your crafting queue in advance

Resource Management

Balance: Maintain proper resource ratios for continuous progression.

  • Always keep emergency tool materials
  • Don't waste materials on unnecessary items
  • Focus on upgrading tools progressively
  • Save rare materials for high-impact items
