Критически важная информация для начала

⚠️ НИКОГДА НЕ ВЫБРАСЫВАЙТЕ НАЧАЛЬНЫЕ КАМНИ!

Вы появляетесь с 2 камнями - они абсолютно необходимы для изготовления первого топора. Без них ваше выживание становится крайне сложным.

Первый приоритет

Отключите блокировщики рекламы для получения дополнительных ресурсов после смерти и готовьтесь потратить минимум 1+ час для значимого прогресса.

Начальные ресурсы

Ваши 2 начальных камня + дерево = ваш первый топор. Этот инструмент необходим для эффективного сбора дерева.

First 30 Minutes Strategy

1

Gather Wood (3-4 pieces)

Immediately start collecting wood from trees. You need this to craft your essential hatchet tool.

2

Craft Hatchet

Use your 2 starting stones + wood to create a hatchet. This makes wood gathering much more efficient.

3

Collect More Resources

Gather more stone and wood for your workbench. Look for oranges and tomatoes for food.

4

Build Workbench

Craft a workbench to unlock more crafting recipes and advanced tools.

5

Make Stone Pickaxe

Craft a stone pickaxe for mining stone and iron ore more efficiently.

6

Build Campfire

Especially important if spawning at night. Cook meat and stay warm.

Essential Survival Mechanics

Food, Water, Energy

Keep these bars above zero or you'll rapidly lose HP. Find oranges, tomatoes, and hunt animals for food.

Tips:

  • Always cook meat before eating
  • Raw meat damages your health
  • Food rots over time - eat fresh

Temperature

Stay warm during night cycles with fires and proper clothing. Cold weather drains your health quickly.

Tips:

  • Build campfires for warmth
  • Craft warm clothing
  • Seek shelter at night

Radiation

Protect yourself in irradiated zones with proper gear. Radiation zones contain valuable loot but are extremely dangerous.

Tips:

  • Craft radiation protection gear
  • Use radiation potions
  • Limit time in irradiated areas

Early Resource Targets

Basic Materials

  • Wood: Essential for all early crafting
  • Stone: Required for tools and building
  • Iron Ore: Needed for advanced tools

Food Sources

  • Oranges: Natural food source
  • Tomatoes: Another natural food option
  • Animal Meat: Must be cooked before eating

Crafting Essentials

  • Keep minimum 200 wood at all times
  • Keep minimum 150 stone at all times
  • Iron is crucial for mid-game progression

Essential Early Crafts

Hatchet

Recipe: 2 Stone + Wood

Wood gathering tool - absolutely essential for efficient resource collection.

Stone Pickaxe

Recipe: Stone + Wood

Mining tool for stone and iron ore. Also decent for melee combat.

Workbench

Recipe: Wood + Stone

Unlocks more crafting recipes and advanced tools. Build this early!

Campfire

Recipe: Wood + Stone

Cook food and stay warm. Essential for survival especially at night.

Common Beginner Mistakes

Dropping Starting Stones

Never drop your 2 starting stones! They're essential for your first hatchet.

Building Wood Walls

Wood walls are too weak against ghouls. Always use stone walls for defense.

Poor Food Storage

Food rots over time. Cook meat immediately and store in firepits as "fridges".

Building in Houses

Building inside houses angers other players and makes you a target.

Ignoring Temperature

Cold nights can kill you quickly. Always prepare warmth sources.

Going Solo Too Long

Late game is extremely difficult alone. Consider forming alliances.

Pro Beginner Tips

Firepit Fridge: Build firepits as "fridges" - cooked meat never rots in them!

Easy XP: Mine stone consistently for easy XP even when well-supplied with resources.

Team Up: Multiple workbenches speed up team crafting significantly.

Day/Night Cycle: Watch the day/night cycle and prepare for temperature drops.

Form Alliances: Survival is much easier in teams - cooperation is key!
